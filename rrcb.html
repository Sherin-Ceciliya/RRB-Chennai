Railway Recruitment Control Board (RRCB)

Railway Recruitment Control Board (RRCB) was set up in 1998 in the Ministry of Railways (Railway Board) with the objectives of:

  • Formulation of policy in regard to recruitment procedures
  • Monitoring the activities of all Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) including recruitment expenditures
  • Evaluation of RRB performance and providing advice on priorities
  • Organising a management information system to monitor work by RRBs
