Welcome to the Recruitment Portal

This portal provides all the latest updates regarding recruitments conducted by the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB), Chennai. We aim to ensure transparent and efficient recruitment procedures in accordance with the policies of the Indian Railways.

Recruitment Rules

All appointments made through this board will strictly adhere to the guidelines laid down by the Ministry of Railways. Candidates are advised to carefully read the notification and ensure they meet all eligibility criteria before applying. No deviation from the eligibility conditions will be permitted.
