Candidates should invariably bring all original certificates in support of their date of birth, qualification, caste (as per the specified proforma), PWD, NOC(Serving Railway /Govt /Public sector Employees), Exs, Minority Affidavit (if applicable), Income Certificate (if applicable) etc., while attending verification.
